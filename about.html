About PriMeHoUr

Where time meets luxury. Discover our journey, our values, and our passion for timeless craftsmanship.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, PriMeHoUr was born from a simple idea: to bring the art of fine watchmaking to those who appreciate elegance, precision, and innovation. What started as a small boutique has now grown into a renowned brand, trusted by thousands of watch enthusiasts worldwide.

Our journey began with a passion for horology and a commitment to quality. Each ChronoLux timepiece is a blend of classic design and modern technology, crafted to be both a statement and a companion for life’s most memorable moments.

Our Mission

To inspire confidence and sophistication through exceptional timepieces that stand the test of time. We believe a great watch is more than an accessory—it’s a legacy.
